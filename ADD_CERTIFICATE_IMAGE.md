## üì∏ **Getting Your Certificate Image**

### **From NPTEL Website:**
1. Log in to NPTEL
2. Go to your certificates
3. Open the LLM certificate
4. Take a screenshot (Windows: `Win + Shift + S`)
5. Save as `llm-certificate.jpg`

### **From Email:**
1. Find NPTEL certificate email
2. Download certificate attachment
3. Rename to `llm-certificate.jpg`
4. Copy to `public/certificates/`

### **From Screenshot:**
1. Open your certificate (the image you showed me)
2. Press `Win + Shift + S` to take screenshot
3. Select the entire certificate
4. Save as `llm-certificate.jpg`
5. Copy to `public/certificates/`
